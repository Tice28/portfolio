How did I get into programming?

During high school, I had no idea what I wanted to do with my life. I thought I would follow my dad and go into engineering or follow my mom and enter the medical field. However, all that changed my senior year of high school when my school got it's first programming course offered.

I signed up and was instantly hooked. It was just an introductory python course, but it sparked my interest in computers and the many ways you can manipulate and instruct them to perform instructions you want them to do.

From there, I went to college for computer science and I graduated cum-laude in May of 2024, and that leads me to where I am today.
